About Us

The Simi Valley handyman you can actually trust to show up, do the job right, and give you a straight answer on pricing.

Local & Reliable Home Repairs

At Simi Valley Handyman Co., we understand how frustrating it is to deal with contractors who don't return calls, show up late, or hit you with surprise invoices when the job is done. We started this business to be the exact opposite.

We are a locally owned, independent handyman service focused exclusively on Simi Valley, Wood Ranch, Moorpark, Thousand Oaks, and Chatsworth. When you call us, you aren't talking to a national lead-generation call center — you're talking directly to the person who is going to show up at your door to fix your home.

Why Choose Us?

  • Upfront Pricing: We give you a real number before we start, not a vague estimate that balloons later.
  • Licensed-Scope & Honest: We operate strictly within California's unlicensed-handyman limits ($500 per job max, labor + materials). We never take on jobs outside our legal scope. If your job requires a licensed contractor, plumber, or electrician, we'll tell you straight up.
  • Same-Day Response: For urgent repairs like a running toilet, a leaky sink, or a broken gate, we strive to offer same-day service availability.
  • Workmanship Guarantee: We stand by our work. If something we fix breaks within 90 days, we'll come back and make it right at no extra charge.

Built for Simi Valley Homes

Houses in Simi Valley face unique challenges: hard water that eats through plumbing fixtures, Santa Ana winds that tear down fences and gutters, and earthquake fault lines that make securing heavy furniture a priority. We know these local issues inside and out, and we bring the right tools and solutions for the California climate.

We believe in doing good work, leaving the workspace cleaner than we found it, and treating your home with respect. It's really that simple.
