Privacy Policy

July 10, 2026

1. What information we collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us directly via our estimate form, including your name, phone number, email address, service requested, neighborhood, and any message you include. We also collect basic analytics data (such as IP address, browsing behavior, and device/browser data) to help us improve our website.

2. How we use it

We use the information you provide strictly to respond to your estimate requests, schedule services, and communicate with you about your project. Your personal information is not sold to third parties.

3. Cookies and tracking

We use cookies to enable basic website functionality and to track website analytics. These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our site so we can improve the user experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

4. California Consumer Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

As a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected about you, the right to request the deletion of your personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information. Simi Valley Handyman Co. does not sell your personal information. To exercise your privacy rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

5. Third-party services

We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and business. These may include our hosting provider, analytics tools, and Google Analytics and Vercel hosting.

6. Data retention

We retain submitted contact form data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

7. How to contact us about privacy

If you have any questions or requests regarding your privacy or this policy, please contact us at: info@simivalleyhandymanco.com

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "last updated" date.
