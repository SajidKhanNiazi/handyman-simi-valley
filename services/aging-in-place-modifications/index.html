Why These Small Changes Matter

Most falls at home happen around a handful of predictable spots — bathrooms, stairs, and poorly lit hallways. None of the fixes above are complicated or expensive on their own, but addressing them before they cause a problem is a lot better than addressing them after.

Proper Installation Matters

Is a grab bar installation a quick job, or does it require special work? It's a quick, standard job when done correctly — the key is anchoring it into a wall stud or using proper heavy-duty anchors rated for real body weight, not just any wall anchor. This is exactly the kind of install where doing it right the first time matters, since it's meant to hold weight in a moment that counts.

What's Outside Our Scope

Full bathroom remodels, widening doorways, walk-in tub or shower conversions, and stairlift installation are larger, often structural or licensed-contractor jobs — outside standard handyman scope. We're glad to help with the smaller, high-impact fixes above, and we'll tell you honestly if what you're describing needs a bigger project and a licensed contractor instead.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you install a grab bar in a fiberglass or tile shower?

Yes — the installation method differs slightly by surface (tile vs. fiberglass), and we'll use the right anchoring approach for your specific bathroom.

How many grab bars does a typical bathroom need?

It depends on the layout and what's most useful for your situation — commonly one near the toilet and one or two in the shower/tub area. We can walk through placement with you during the visit.

Do you install ramps?

Small, non-structural threshold ramps for minor height differences, yes. Full exterior entry ramps are typically a larger project outside standard handyman scope — we'll let you know which situation applies.

Is this service only for elderly homeowners?

No — these modifications are useful for anyone recovering from an injury, managing a temporary mobility limitation, or simply wanting a safer home, not just older homeowners.
